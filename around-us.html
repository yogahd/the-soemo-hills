Padusan Pool

By Agil Fathiha Aulia Rahmi | Uploaded on May 24, 2025


This tour is in the Wana Wisata Padusan Pacet area. This tour is situated at an altitude of approximately 800 meters above sea level, under the foot of Mount Welirang. The cool atmosphere of the mountains combined with the warmth of natural sulfur water makes this place suitable for unwinding and refreshing your family's body.

The main attraction is the hot spring pool that comes from the natural sulfur source of Mount Welirang. This hot water is believed to have many benefits, such as relieving aches and pains, treating minor skin diseases, and improving blood circulation. Visitors can enjoy the public wading pool or rent a private pool for more comfort.

Some of the facilities that you can enjoy at Padusan Hot Spring Tour include hot and cold swimming pools, sulfur therapy pools, private wading rooms, children's play area, food stalls and souvenir centers, camping ground and outbound area, and a large parking area.

Padusan is particularly busy on weekends and holidays, when both local residents and out-of-town visitors come to enjoy its natural beauty. However, visiting on a weekday can offer a quieter, more intimate experience with nature. Morning visits are highly recommended, as the air is crisp, the pools are less crowded, and the sunrise over the hills is breathtaking.

From Mojokerto City, this hot spring can be reached in about 1-1.5 hours by car or motorcycle. With good road access and the availability of many signposts, this tour can be easily visited by anyone. And interestingly, Padusan Hot Spring is only 100 meters from The Soemo Hills, Pacet if you walk need around 5 minutes. The entrance ticket price is also quite affordable, usually ranging from IDR 15,000 to IDR 25,000 per person, depending on the day and the type of pool chosen.

Starberry Garden

By Agil Fathiha Aulia Rahmi | Uploaded on May 24, 2025


Besides being famous for its hot springs, the Padusan Pacet area in Mojokerto has another interesting tourist destination: the strawberry picking garden! In this place, visitors can experience the thrill of picking fresh strawberries directly from the tree, while enjoying the cool and beautiful mountain atmosphere. Located in the highlands with cool air and fertile soil, Padusan Pacet is a suitable location for strawberry cultivation. The fruit, which usually grows in cold areas such as Lembang or Batu, can now be enjoyed in Pacet Mojokerto.

Visitors are provided with a small container and allowed to pick their own ripe strawberries. The sweet and sour taste of the strawberries is refreshing, perfect for enjoying directly or taking home as souvenirs. This strawberry picking tour is perfect for children and families because it is educational. Children can learn about how to plant and care for strawberries, as well as understand the process from flower to ready-to-harvest fruit.

The best time to visit is during the dry season or early in the morning, when the plants are at their freshest and the paths are easy to walk. Be sure to wear comfortable shoes and bring a hat or umbrella to stay comfortable under the sun or light rain.

Visitors are usually charged a very affordable entrance ticket to enter the strawberry picking area, ranging from IDR 10,000 to IDR 15,000 per person. The access road to this tour is very easy and wide, allowing cars or tour buses to pass. If you are coming from Mojokerto City, your trip will take about 1.5 hours. This place is only 200 meters away from The Soemo Hills Pacet you just need 7 minutes to walk with beautiful scenery.

Grenjengan Waterfall

By Agil Fathiha Aulia Rahmi | Uploaded on May 24, 2025


In the middle of the cool mountainous area of Pacet, Mojokerto, there is a hidden gem that is still natural and not too crowded with tourists, namely Grenjengan Waterfall. Grenjengan Waterfall is located in Padusan Hamlet, Pacet District, Mojokerto. Surrounded by dense trees and natural rocks, the atmosphere around this waterfall is still very calm, perfect for those of you who want to unwind from the hustle and bustle of the city.

To reach the location of this waterfall, visitors need to walk about 10 to 15 minutes through a path and a small forest area. The path is quite challenging, it adds to the impression of adventure for nature lovers. Along the way, you will be treated to green scenery that soothes the eyes, and if you are lucky, you can hear the sounds of wild birds that inhabit the forest area.

Arriving at the waterfall, you can sit on the rocks, play in the water, or simply enjoy the silence of nature. Many visitors call this place suitable for “natural healing.”

Entrance tickets to Grenjengan Waterfall are usually very affordable, ranging from IDR 5,000 - IDR 10,000 per person. Since it is still a natural tourism site that has not been commercialized much, the facilities here are quite simple but that is precisely the attraction for lovers of silence and wild nature.

Some of the basic facilities available: a parking area, a local stall near the entrance, a fairly safe path, a simple gazebo for resting. The waterfall is only 400 meters away from The Soemo Hills Pacet.

Coban Canggu Waterfall

By Agil Fathiha Aulia Rahmi | Uploaded on May 24, 2025


Coban Canggu Waterfall offers a peaceful retreat for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and anyone craving a quiet moment away from the city. With its lush surroundings, fresh mountain air, and the calming sound of falling water, this waterfall is one of East Java’s best kept natural treasures.

Coban Canggu is located in the village of Padusan, about 850 meters from The Soemo Hills. The journey to get there is part of the adventure, with winding roads through hills, pine forests, and rice terraces that give you a taste of the area’s natural beauty. Once you arrive at the entrance, a short trek through a scenic footpath leads you to the waterfall. The trail is relatively easy and well-maintained, making it accessible even for beginners and families.

The waterfall itself is around 30 meters tall, cascading down a cliff into a shallow, clear pool surrounded by mossy rocks and thick greenery. The cool mist that rises from the base of the falls adds to the serene atmosphere, making it a great spot to relax, meditate, or just soak in the natural beauty. For the more adventurous, the area around the waterfall also offers opportunities for light exploration, photography, or even a refreshing splash in the natural pool.

There are basic facilities available near the entrance, including parking, small food stalls, and rest areas. However, it’s recommended to bring your water, snacks, and suitable footwear for the walk. Early morning visits are ideal, as the air is crisp and the light shines beautifully through the trees. Visitors need to walk about 15 to 20 minutes. Entrance tickets to Grenjengan Waterfall are usually very affordable, ranging from IDR 7,000 to IDR 10,000 per person.

Ubalan Waterpark

By Agil Fathiha Aulia Rahmi | Uploaded on May 24, 2025


Tucked away in the cool highlands of Pacet, Mojokerto, Ubalan Swimming Pool is a refreshing destination perfect for families, couples, or anyone looking to relax and enjoy the fresh mountain air. Surrounded by lush greenery and set against a backdrop of rolling hills, this water recreation park is a local favorite, offering natural spring water pools and a relaxing atmosphere for all ages.

Thanks to its elevation at around 800 meters above sea level, the air in Pacet stays pleasantly cool throughout the year. The pools at Ubalan are filled with natural spring water straight from the nearby mountains, making every swim feel fresh, crisp, and invigorating. Whether you want to take a dip, float your stress away, or let the kids splash around in safety, Ubalan has the right pool for everyone.

This isn’t just a standard swimming pool. Ubalan offers a variety of areas tailored to different needs, including a large adult pool for adult swimmers, a children’s pool complete with shallow areas and fun slides that add excitement to the experience. The layout of the park blends well with the surrounding nature, with shaded spots, garden paths, and seating areas that let you feel comfortable in the peaceful vibes.

Ubalan Swimming Pool is also equipped with family-friendly facilities. Visitors can find changing rooms, toilets, gazebos for relaxing, and even a small lake nearby where paddle boats are available for rent. On weekends or holidays, there may also be live music or entertainment, adding to the cheerful and lively atmosphere. Small food stalls and traditional snacks are available on-site, giving you a taste of local flavors without having to leave the area.

One of the best things about Ubalan is that it’s incredibly affordable. Entry fee is IDR 30,000, making it an ideal choice for a budget-friendly outing. Its location is also convenient, easily accessible by car from Mojokerto city, and just a short drive from other popular tourist spots like the hot springs at Padusan, the waterfalls in the area, and the increasingly popular resort The Soemo Hills, around 1 kilometer away, and takes 5 minutes to drive.
